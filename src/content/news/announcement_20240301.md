Bego joined the group as a PhD student.

She is funded by CRUK, co-supervised by 
[Peter Hall](https://edwebprofiles.ed.ac.uk/profile/peter-hall), 
[Colin McLean](https://edwebprofiles.ed.ac.uk/profile/cmclean5) and 
[Joanne Edwards](https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/cancersciences/staff/joanneedwards/). 

Her project focuses on risk prediction modelling in the context of colorectal
cancer, whilst also focusing on methodological aspects of survival analysis and
competing risks.

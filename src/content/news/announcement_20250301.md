Victor Velasco-Pardo joined the group as a Postdoctoral Researcher. Victor has 
also joined the group led by [Charlie Lees](https://institute-genetics-cancer.ed.ac.uk/research/research-groups-a-z/lees-group). 

Victor will be embedded in a cross-disciplinary team, working alongside data
scientists and clinical colleagues. His project will focus on dynamic risk 
prediction and its application to inflammatory bowel disease.

Catalina Vallejos was appointed as one of the Co-Directors for the 
[Health Data Research UK (HDRUK) Scotland Regional Network](https://www.hdruk.ac.uk/research/regional-networks/health-data-research-uk-hdr-uk-scotland/). 

In this role, Cata will work alongside 
[Katie Wilde (University of Aberdeen)](https://www.hdruk.ac.uk/people/katie-wilde/) and
[Honghan Wu (University of Glasgow)](https://www.hdruk.ac.uk/people/dr-honghan-wu/),
setting the strategy and direction for the network, supporting the health data
science community across Scotland.

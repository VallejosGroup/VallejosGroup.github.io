Andrew Papanastasiou (XDF fellow) has been awarded funds from the [Wellcome 
Institutional Strategic Support Fund (ISSF)](https://wellcome.org/research-funding/funding-portfolio/institutional-strategic-support-fund) 
to perform a single-cell multiomics study of eye organoids. The project will be 
performed in collaboration with Dr. Catalina Vallejos and Prof. David FitzPatrick.

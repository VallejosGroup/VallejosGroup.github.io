Elliot Gemmell is a clinical PhD student. Alex Rudge is a postdoctoral fellow.

<img src = "/assets/img/group-members/becky.webp" width = "200">

This month is also the official start of Becky Gordon's PhD project, funded by
[HDR UK-Turing Wellcome PhD Programme in Health Data Science](https://www.hdruk.ac.uk/study-and-train/study/phd/hdr-uk-turing-wellcome-phd-programme-in-health-data-science/).
She will be co-supervised by [Dorien Kimenai](https://www.research.ed.ac.uk/en/persons/dorien-kimenai/), 
[Neeraj Dhaun (Bean)](https://edwebprofiles.ed.ac.uk/profile/neeraj-dhaun) and 
[Peter Gallacher](https://edinburghkidney.co.uk/ournetwork/peter-gallacher/). 
Becky's project will focus on dynamic risk prediction in the context of chronic
kidney disease.
